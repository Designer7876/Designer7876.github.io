A brief view on some types of Martial Arts styles

  • The meaning of Martial Arts:

    Martial Arts has ever been thought of a way to maintain peace and prosperity. Many great martial artists have honed their skills in order to teach the new generation the way to maintain peace. Martial artisits also practice their respective arts because:

    1. It teaches honor and respect.
    2. It teaches you to use non-violent conflict resolution.
    3. It helps you to set and achieve goals.
    4. It helps you to gain confidence and boost self-esteem.
    5. It improves your mental and physical strength.
